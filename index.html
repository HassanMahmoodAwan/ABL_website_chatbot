myABL Wallet

myABL Wallet app is a mobile financial service to cater the needs of the unbanked segment of Pakistan. This service provides a branchless banking account which is associated with your mobile number. Any user with a mobile phone can securely send and receive money, pay bills, educational fees, make donations, do mobile top-ups and much more.

myABL WhatsApp Banking

Allied Bank introduces myABL WhatsApp Banking which offers financial transactions and inquires to all of its customers. No need to visit any ABL Branch or ATM for Balance Inquiry or Mini Statement. Simply save myABL WhatsApp number 0300 1225225 in your contact list and immediately start chatting with us. Communication is encrypted from end-to-end to guarantee the privacy and safety of our customers.
